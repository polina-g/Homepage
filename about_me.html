About Me

I am an aspiring fullstack developer, career changer and full-time student from Boston, Massachusetts.

Not too long ago I thought I had life figured out: I was married (still am), working as a behavioral clinician in a comfortable, but challenging role, and slowly figuring out what I need to do to get my LMHC license.

One day at the beginning of 2021, I decided to try my hand at coding. Not knowing where to staft, I dove into the Free Code Camp online course, starting with HTML, CSS and Javascript. That was the day my life started start to change: I fell in love with coding.

Fast forward some months later, I found myself quitting my job and applying to a full-time immersive software engineer programm at General Assembly. My cohort begins in September and I can hardly wait! I am so excited to take control of my future and dive into this new world that I have already fallen in love with.
